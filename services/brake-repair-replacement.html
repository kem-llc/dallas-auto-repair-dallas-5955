Common questions

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Typically every 30,000 to 70,000 miles, but it depends on driving habits and vehicle type. We recommend an inspection every 10,000 miles.

What are signs of brake trouble?

Squealing or grinding noises, a soft brake pedal, vibration when braking, or the brake warning light on your dashboard.
