Our story

Dallas Auto Repair: Your Local Auto Care Experts

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road. We believe in transparency, integrity, and exceptional craftsmanship.

Dallas Auto Repair was founded over two decades ago with a simple mission: offer reliable car repairs at fair prices. What started as a small two-bay shop on Buckner Blvd has grown into a trusted destination for drivers across Dallas. Our founder, a lifelong mechanic, built the business on the principle that every customer deserves to understand what's happening with their car and why.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians continues that tradition. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to service modern vehicles effectively. But we haven't forgotten the personal touch — we still greet every customer by name, explain repairs in plain language, and stand behind our work with a solid warranty. Whether you need a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, we treat your car as if it were our own.
